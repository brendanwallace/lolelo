What is it?

lolelo.gg is a website that makes predictions about the NA LCS using the Elo rating system.

How does it work?

The elo system determines a rating for every team, and the difference between two teams' ratings gives a prediction of how likely each team is to win. Roughly: for each 400 points' difference, the lower rated team is 10 times less likely to win.

The predictions (e.g. how likely a team is to qualify for Worlds) are made by randomly simulating all the unplayed matches in the season a bunch of times, and seeing how many times each outcome happened. Divide that number by the number of times everything was simulated and you end up with roughly how likely something is to happen.
